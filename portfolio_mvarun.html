Projects & Ideas

Building toward a future where technology creates positive impact.

MindBridge

A mental health accessibility platform designed for individuals who may not have easy access to traditional support systems. MindBridge combines AI-guided journaling, mood tracking, self-reflection tools, and educational resources to help users better understand themselves. The vision is not to replace therapy— but to make support more approachable and accessible.

ClearCare

A healthcare transparency platform helping patients better understand treatments, medical terminology, and healthcare systems through simpler communication.
